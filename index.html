About the Workshop

The 5th BioMEMS and Microfluidic Technologies Workshop is designed to promote interdisciplinary discussion across engineering, nanotechnology, biomedical sciences, and point-of-care innovation.

Workshop Overview

This workshop aims to create a collaborative platform for scientists, engineers, and clinicians interested in miniaturized systems for diagnostics, sensing, biological analysis, and next-generation healthcare technologies.

It will include invited talks, thematic sessions, academic networking opportunities, and discussions on emerging directions in micro- and nanoscale biomedical technologies.

Abstract Submission

Researchers and graduate students are invited to submit abstracts presenting recent advances in BioMEMS, microfluidics, biosensors, and related biomedical microsystems. Accepted abstracts will be presented as posters or short oral presentations.

Submission Guidelines

• Abstract length: maximum 300 words

• Topics must align with workshop themes

• Submissions should be in English

• Selected abstracts may be invited for short oral presentations
